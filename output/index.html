Welcome to dns.bentasker.co.uk

A small ad and phishing blocking DNS privacy resolver supporting D-o-H and D-o-T

My DNS service uses various well known ad-blocking lists in order to block ads, along with adblocking lists of my own, along with the Phishing Army anti-Phishing lists. See Blocklists and Safety for more information.

This service is, at best, a small hobby project to ensure that there are still some privacy-sensitive DNS services out there. See my Privacy Policy and Abuse page for details of what limited data is recorded, and when it might be passed onto third parties.

This service has resolvers in locations across the globe.

See Setup for details on the configuration required to use my DNS Privacy service

Features Overview

  • Minimal Logging
  • DNSSEC
  • ECS
  • Upstream name minimisation
  • DoT/DoH
